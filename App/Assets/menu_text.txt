STRATEGIC WAR FRONT: MALABAR AND BEYOND
The current state of the Malabar Front is doubleplusgood. Our glorious forces have successfully liquidated the Eurasian 14th Division near the southern delta. Casualties are non-existent. The enemy retreated in total ungood disorder, abandoning heavy munitions and floating fortresses. Big Brother has expressed his profound satisfaction with the bravery of the 5th Air Fleet. All citizens are reminded that victory is the only possible outcome of the current engagement. We have always been at war with Eurasia. Reports to the contrary are to be reported to the nearest patrol of the Thought Police for immediate rectification. The 1978 treaties are now declared null-void-unhistory. 

MINIPLENTY PRODUCTION ADVISORY: RATION RECTIFICATION
Attention, workers of Airstrip One! The Ministry of Plenty is proud to announce that the chocolate ration has been raised to twenty grams per week. This is a monumental increase from the previous standard. Furthermore, the production of synthetic gin has reached 114% of the quarterly forecast. The footwear shortage of last month has been revealed as a malicious rumor spread by Goldstein’s agents. In reality, the production of boots has reached 15 million pairs, ensuring every citizen of the Party is equipped for the march toward industrial perfection. Total efficiency is up. Total hunger is down. Any worker observing a lack of supply in their local canteen is directed to file a 'Gratitude Form' rather than a complaint.

SOCIAL ARCHIVE CORRECTION: RE-INDEXING OF UNPERSONS
Refer to Document 1984/AF/92. The individual previously known as Comrade Withers has become an unperson. He does not exist. He never existed. All references to his participation in the 1978 Arctic Expedition are to be deleted. The record must now reflect that the expedition was led by Comrade Ogilvy, whose heroic death in battle last month saved the 3rd Labor Battalion from a sabotage attempt. Ogilvy was a model of Ingsoc loyalty. At the age of three, he refused all toys except a drum, a sub-machine gun, and a model helicopter. At eleven, he denounced his uncle to the Thought Police. He was a true child of the Party. He is dead, but he is more real than those who betray the State.

MANDATORY SENTIMENT GUIDELINES: EMOTIONAL STOICISM
The Two Minutes Hate will commence at 11:00 hours in all sectors. Attendance is not optional. Failure to display visible enthusiasm during the screening of the arch-traitor Goldstein will be noted by the telescreens. Remember: thoughtcrime is death. Thoughtcrime does not entail death; thoughtcrime IS death. Keep your face a blank canvas of loyalty. Do not allow your features to betray a flicker of unorthodoxy. The Ministry of Love reminds you that Big Brother is watching you. Watch your pulse. Watch your breathing. Even a twitch of the eye is a betrayal of the collective.

LINGUISTIC REFINEMENT NOTICE: THE ELEVENTH EDITION
The Eleventh Edition of the Newspeak Dictionary is nearing completion. We are successfully cutting the language down to the bone. The beauty of the destruction of words is a thing of majesty. By 2050, no human being will be able to understand a conversation like this one, because the concepts of freedom and rebellion will have no words to describe them. Every year, fewer and fewer words, and the range of consciousness always a little smaller. Duckspeak is the goal. To speak without thinking. To bark like a dog from the larynx without involving the brain. This is the ultimate state of orthodoxy.

MINIPAX DEFENSE UPDATE: ROCKET BOMB ALERTS
Rocket bombs have fallen on the prole quarters in the East End today. Preliminary investigations by the Ministry of Peace suggest these were fired by Eurasian subversives located in the Atlantic territories. Twenty citizens were martyred for the Cause. Their names have been added to the Eternal Ledger. We will retaliate with doubleplus intensity. The sky will rain fire upon the enemies of Ingsoc. Citizens are encouraged to ignore the sirens and continue their shifts. Interruption of labor is a gift to the enemy. Work is safety.

INTERNAL MONITORING: THE BROTHERHOOD THREAT
The Thought Police have intercepted several encrypted communications originating from the dark corners of the city. These messages claim that a secret organization known as The Brotherhood is active. This is a lie. There is no Brotherhood. There is only Goldstein's madness. If you see a citizen acting strangely—reading old books, walking alone at night, or failing to attend the communal hikes—report them immediately. Vaporization is the only cure for the infection of doubt.

JUNIOR SPIES DIVISION: TRIUMPH OF VIGILANCE
The Ministry of Love is doubleplusproud to announce a record-breaking month for the Youth League Spies. Special commendation is given to Child 112-Q, who observed his father attempting to conceal an old-world silver coin beneath a floorboard. The father has been taken for rectification; the child has been awarded the 'Medal of State Fidelity.' This is the true spirit of Ingsoc. The family unit is a secondary construct; the Party is the eternal parent.

MINIPLENTY INFRASTRUCTURE UPDATE: THE NEW LONDON BLOCKS
Construction of the Victory Apartments in the southern industrial zone is proceeding at 140% of the scheduled pace. These new dwellings are designed with total transparency in mind—the new telescreen models are larger, louder, and capable of monitoring heartbeat irregularities from across a room. This is for your safety. A citizen with nothing to hide has nothing to fear from the constant gaze of the State. Furthermore, the communal plumbing issues reported last week were a figment of oldthink. The water is flowing perfectly. Any rust observed in the pipes is actually a new mineral supplement added by the Ministry of Health to improve worker stamina.

HISTORICAL RECTIFICATION: THE 1962 FLOODS
Refer to Archive 002/TX. It has come to light that the 'Great Flood' of 1962 was malquoted in previous editions. New evidence proves that the flood never occurred. The rainfall was actually a controlled irrigation experiment conducted by the Ministry of Plenty to increase algae yields. Any citizen claiming to 'remember' a disaster is suffering from a memory-leak and should report to the nearest clinic for a chemical reset. The past is a blank book; the Party holds the pen. We control the weather, we control the memory, we control the truth.

CULTURAL PURITY INITIATIVE: THE END OF ART
The Department of Aesthetic Purity has successfully incinerated the remaining cache of 'Romantic' landscape paintings found in the ruins of the Old Gallery. In their place, the Party has commissioned three million copies of 'Worker 49, Smiling at a Lathe.' This is the only art a loyal citizen requires. The concept of 'beauty' is being redefined to mean 'maximum industrial output.' Anything else is a symptom of ideological decay.

MINIPAX STRATEGIC UPDATE: THE FLOATING FORTRESSES
Reports from the front lines in the Sahara show that our new Floating Fortresses have rendered the Eurasian defensive lines obsolete. The enemy is currently begging for a ceasefire, which we have rightfully ignored. There can be no peace until every square inch of the globe is under the benevolent shadow of Big Brother. We have never been in alliance with Eurasia. We have always been at war with Eurasia. The rumors of a 'secret treaty' are the desperate lies of the traitor Goldstein. He is hiding in the shadows, but the light of Ingsoc will find him.

The destruction of 'Oldthink' adjectives is proceeding at a magnificent rate. The final goal of Newspeak is to make the human voice indistinguishable from the quack of a duck—pure, rhythmic, thoughtless loyalty.
